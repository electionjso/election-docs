# Election Guidelines

## 1️⃣ Membership Finalization

- Announce a membership cut-off date.
- Publish a provisional voter list.
- Allow a 3–5 day window for corrections.
- Publish the final certified voter list.

> **Note:** Each member has one vote under Article 10.

## 2️⃣ Clarification of Scope of Election

- Elections for Global Council (GC)?
- Elections also for Regional/National/Chapter Councils?

> This decision must be recorded in official minutes.
