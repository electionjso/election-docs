Meeting 4
=========================

Election & Nomination Committee (ENC) – Meeting 4
=================================================

:Date: 29 March 2026

Meeting Purpose
---------------

To initiate planning for the JSO 2026 election process and discuss preparatory steps required for conducting the elections.

Topics
------

Election Scope & Structure
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

- **Problem:** The current constitution grants all members voting rights up to the Global Council, which risks low-information voters electing unqualified leaders.
- **Solution:** A "step-by-step" election model will filter candidates:

  1. **Chapter/National Council:** All members can vote and run.
  2. **Regional/Global Council:** Only elected National Council members can vote and run.

- **Rationale:** This structure ensures leaders are proven contributors.

National Council as a Filter
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

- **Function:** The National Council will serve as the primary filter for higher-level leadership.
- **Composition:**

  - **Strong Chapters (e.g., USA):** Can form independent councils.
  - **Weak Chapters (e.g., Singapore):** Will be grouped into regional councils (e.g., "Far East").

- **Representation:** The goal is empowerment, not country strength. A smaller country group could have more National Council members than a single, larger country if it has more active volunteers.

Constitutional & Timeline Conflicts
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

- **Application Window:** The 40 business days application window is too long for the current organization size and must be shortened.
- **Voting Rights:** The constitution's general member voting rights conflict directly with the proposed step-by-step model and require amendment.
- **Membership Cutoff:** The cutoff will be moved to one week before the election to encourage new member sign-ups.

Nomination & Communication
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

- **Process:** A single nomination form will allow candidates to declare interest in multiple councils (Chapter, National, Regional, Global) simultaneously.
- **Communication:** Rama will create zone-specific email groups for targeted election announcements and voting instructions.

- **Rama:** Create zone-specific email groups for election communications.
- **Committee:** Finalize the nomination form and technical voting details (platform, duration) after the committee review.
