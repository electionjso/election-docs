Meeting 2
=========================

Election & Nomination Committee (ENC) – Meeting 2
=================================================

:Date: 14 February 2026

Meeting Purpose
---------------

To initiate planning for the JSO 2026 election process and discuss preparatory steps required for conducting the elections.

Key Takeaways
-------------

- Continue enhancing the technical capabilities (GitHub repository) to support the Election & Nomination Committee.
- Conduct a membership drive prior to the elections to ensure an updated member database.
- Invite Ramesh ji to provide guidance on the dependency between constitutional amendments and the election process, and to advise on the way forward.

Discussion Points
-----------------

Technical Support for Elections
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

- Rama Shankar demonstrated the capabilities of GitHub in maintaining a centralized repository of documents for future reference.
- The repository can also support the development of an LLM-based tool trained on the JSO Constitution and related documents, allowing members to quickly retrieve accurate information when queried.
- Rama Shankar will continue developing the repository. For this purpose, the constitutional document and membership details may be required.

Membership Drive
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

- Swami has re-initiated the JSO membership drive, with a deadline of 21 March 2026.
- Chapter leads will circulate the membership message within their respective WhatsApp groups, encouraging members to complete the membership form.

Constitutional Amendments
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

- It was noted that, as per the constitution, the General Body may need to convene and dissolve all current roles prior to elections.
- However, this may create operational challenges, as certain individuals would still be required to manage the election process and ongoing initiatives.
- ENC members agreed that there may be a dependency on constitutional amendments prior to conducting the elections.
- To seek further guidance, Ramesh ji will be invited to the next ENC meeting to advise on how the committee can proceed without causing significant delays.

Next Steps
----------

**Swami**

- Obtain the membership list from Atul, including details of members who have received certificates and those whose certificates are pending.
- Source the signed or latest version of the constitution in Word format for upload to the GitHub repository.
- Invite Ramesh ji to participate in the next ENC meeting.
- Continue to promote the membership drive until the deadline of 21 March 2026.

**Rama Shankar**

- Provide an update on technical progress at the next meeting.
- Facilitate GitHub access for ENC members.
