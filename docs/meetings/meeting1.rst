Election & Nomination Committee (ENC) – Meeting 1
=================================================

:Date: 28 February 2026

Meeting Purpose
---------------

To plan the JSO 2026 election process.

Key Takeaways
-------------

- **Membership is the First Step:** A 1 to 2 week window will be opened for all active contributors to formally register as members, establishing the official voter list before the election process begins.
- **Two-Tier Election Proposed:** A new structure was proposed to ensure fairness and accountability: direct elections for country/regional heads, who then indirectly elect the Global Council (GC) and its leadership.
- **GitHub for Documentation:** A private GitHub repository will serve as the central, secure record-keeping system for all election documents, including member certificates and nomination forms, with a formal review and approval workflow.
- **Constitutional Amendments Required:** The proposed two-tier election structure and new eligibility criteria (e.g., minimum one-year tenure) will require formal amendments to the JSO Constitution.

Topics
------

Membership & Voter List
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

- **Problem:** Many active contributors are not formal members, creating an unclear voter list and potential for disruption.
- **Solution:** A 1–2 week window will be opened for all active contributors to formally register as members.

  - **Rationale:** This establishes a clear, official voter list before the election process begins.

- **Process:**

  - The Tech Group will manage the registration form and issue certificates.
  - This group will screen applicants and has the authority to disqualify members for disruptive behaviour.
  - Vivek will handle orientation for new members (consent to be obtained).

Election Structure & Voting
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

- **Problem:** The current constitution's election process may not be scalable or fair for a global organization.
- **Proposed Solution:** Two-Tier Election

  - **Tier 1 (Direct):** All members in a country/region directly elect their local leadership (e.g., US President, Canada Head).
  - **Tier 2 (Indirect):** The newly elected country/regional heads then indirectly elect the Global Council (GC) and its leadership (President, Secretary, Treasurer).

- **Rationale:** This model ensures fairness by giving all members a direct vote while creating a more manageable and accountable process for selecting global leadership.
- **Alternative (Rejected):** A fully direct global election was deemed impractical due to the small number of members in many countries.

Candidate Eligibility & Nomination
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

- **Problem:** The current constitution lacks clear criteria, risking unqualified candidates and potential conflicts of interest.
- **Proposed Eligibility Criteria:**

  - **Minimum Tenure:** A candidate must be a member for at least one year to demonstrate commitment.
  - **Role Exclusivity:** A candidate can only run for one position per election.
  - **Performance Metrics:** Candidates for higher roles should have a track record of measurable contributions ("SMART goals").

- **Nomination Process:**

  - Candidates must submit a written application detailing their vision and qualifications.
  - The Election Commission will review all nominations against the new eligibility criteria.

Documentation & Communication
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

- **Problem:** The current reliance on WhatsApp and informal documents is insecure and lacks a central record.
- **Solution:** GitHub Repository

  - A private GitHub repository will serve as the central, secure record-keeping system.
  - **Content:** Member certificates, nomination forms, election results, and all official documents.
  - **Workflow:** All document updates will require formal review and approval from the group before being merged.

- **Solution:** Formal Communication Channels

  - **Email:** The primary channel for official announcements to members.
  - **Google Calendar:** For scheduling all meetings and deadlines.
  - **Meeting Recordings:** Will be provided for members who cannot attend live sessions.
